enum solutionVariable_t

enumeration for the solution variables

this clarifies indexing into solution arrays the variables are as follows:

  • phi_e potential in the electrolyte
  • phi_s potential in the solid
  • conc_e ion concentration in the electrolyte
  • conc_s ion concentration in the solid
  • wallFlux rate of production (or consumption) of ions in between the electrolyte and solid
