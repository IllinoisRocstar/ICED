Solver for the transference number.

The TransferenceNumber SolvePhysics function is a submodule that solves for the fraction of current carried by an ion in a solution of uniform compositions based on the concentration of the electrolyte . The current implementation assume a parabola, but other function could be added. The coefficients of the parabola are read from the input file(s).
