void SolvePhysics ( )

Solves the ConcentrationDiffusion physics.

The ConcentrationDiffusion SolvePhysics function is a submodule that solves for the concentration-dependent diffusion coefficient with units . The diffusion coefficient measures how quickly an ion moves through a solution. It is based on the concentration of electrolyte . The current implementation assumes a parabola, but other functions could be added. The coefficients of the parabola are read from the input file(s).
